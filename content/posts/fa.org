/Hard choices, makes life easier, easy choices makes life harder/

First entry will be special and very personal, because the creation of this blog coincided with drastic changes in my life - the title explains it all - and therefore all other work will be suspended until this problem is resolved.

I didn't know it existed until June this year. Theoretically, there were articles on Wikipedia that he came across, but in the 2008 Polish edition, there was no mention of a disorganized style and I didn't know English at the time, so I lived in ignorance for the rest of my life. 

After a series of countless failures, confusion, mental disorders[fn:0][fn:1][fn:2][fn:3] finally i get rid of.

1. First of all, if you haven't done it yet (I didn't have to, as they didn't have much of an influence on me), forget everything you've seen in pop culture. It have as much to do with the truth as porn with real sex - lies or half-truth at best. Examples: [[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Imb09sotcmU][4 ways in which Hollywood messed up our views on love]]
2. Avoid redpill shit, because it's just like [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Horseshoe_theory][going from extreme to extreme]] and are especially harmful to FA as they fuel your core wound[fn:5]. For example in The Redpill handbook says:
   - Don't trust women. Lying and manipulating is to them what resolve and hard work are for us: our weapons in life.
   - Trust people (even women) to act in their own (perceived) self-interest at all times. You won't be disappointed. By WishIHadMyOldUsernam.
   - How do you trust anyone? Trust yourself, trust your judgement, don't take things personally and don't let others control you. By lonGterMgoalS.
   - Women simply. Are. Not. Loyal.
   - Etc. Confirmation bias is very strong here — they literally promote avoidant attachment. And it's a pseudoscience. Always use primary sources. Don't be fooled. And you guess what? Just because something can happen doesn't mean it will happen. So enjoy it, cherish it.
3. Instead, go to and learn correct attachment style. We are programmed for them by default. Tldr; 
4. If you feel stuck, don't worry. This is not speedrun, just a process. These books are so structured that you can be cured without consulting a psychotherapist. I was very determined to do this for two reasons: first is that in Poland [[https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/statistics-explained/index.php?title=Mental_health_and_related_issues_statistics][we have worst possible care of mental health (9.2 per 100 000 inhabitants) over the EU]]) second, because the core wound is trust issue, there may be a problem with trusting a psychotherapist.
5. The most annoying thing is probably not possible fully cured without finding someone with a secure style. A certain workaround to this problem is to absorb relevant patterns, good examples, which paulien talks about at the end of the video in the first link.
